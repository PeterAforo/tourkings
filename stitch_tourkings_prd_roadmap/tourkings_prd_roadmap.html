# Product Requirements Document: TourKings ## Project Overview TourKings is a Ghanaian-owned travel platform offering tour packages and destinations in Ghana and worldwide. The platform's unique value proposition is its flexible payment system, allowing customers to pay outright or save towards a package over time. ## Target Audience - Travelers seeking curated local and international experiences. - Savings-conscious users who want to plan and fund their dream vacations gradually. - Groups, couples, and solo travelers. ## Key Features 1. **Curated Tour Packages:** Detailed listings for various destinations. 2. **Flexible Payment Options:** Outright purchase or incremental savings. 3. **Automated Savings Alerts:** Notifications when accumulated savings match a package's price. 4. **Contribution Tracking:** A dashboard to monitor savings and manage tour selection. ## Proposed Screens ### 1. Home Screen (Landing Page) - **Objective:** Showcase featured tours, promote the brand story (Ghanaian heritage), and explain the 'Save Towards a Package' feature. - **Key Elements:** Hero section with inspiring travel imagery, featured tour cards (domestic & international), 'How it Works' section for savings, and testimonials. ### 2. Tour Listings (Search & Filter) - **Objective:** Allow users to browse all available tours. - **Key Elements:** Robust filtering (Price, Destination, Type: Group/Couple/Single, Duration), search bar, and card-based tour previews with clear pricing and highlights. ### 3. Tour Detail Page - **Objective:** Provide exhaustive information about a specific package. - **Key Elements:** High-quality image gallery, detailed itinerary, inclusions/exclusions (transportation, amenities, meals), duration, tour type (Single/Couple/Group), and two CTA buttons: 'Pay Outright' and 'Save Towards This Tour'. ### 4. Customer Dashboard (The 'Vault') - **Objective:** The central hub for tracking contributions and managing tours. - **Key Elements:** Total balance display, transaction history, 'Next Goal' tracker showing progress toward a selected package, and an alert section for when goals are met. ### 5. Checkout & Contribution Flow - **Objective:** A seamless process for making payments or contributions. - **Key Elements:** Secure payment gateway integration, recurring contribution setup options, and clear confirmation of funds allocated to the 'Travel Vault'. --- **Next Steps:** Once you've reviewed this plan, I will start by designing the **Home Screen** and the **Tour Detail Page** to establish the core visual language.
